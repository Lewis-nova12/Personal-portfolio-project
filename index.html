About me

Hi, I’m Lewis Mwinga, a passionate web developer from Kenya with a Diploma in Information and Communication Technology (ICT). I love bringing ideas to life through clean, functional, and user-friendly websites. My journey in tech has equipped me with skills in a variety of programming languages, including C, C++, Visual Basic, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Web development is where my heart lies, and I’m always exploring new ways to create seamless digital experiences. Whether it’s crafting responsive designs or building dynamic web applications, I’m driven by a curiosity for technology and a commitment to creating solutions that make an impact. When I’m not coding, you might find me vibing to some classic tunes or diving into new tech trends. Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
